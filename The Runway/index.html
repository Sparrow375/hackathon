FLIGHT DETAILS

Everything you need to know about The Runway

01

The Mission

The Runway is a one-day innovation and entrepreneurship event designed to encourage students to develop creative solutions, build prototypes, and present their ideas in a startup-style pitching environment.

Participants will submit problem statements, develop prototypes, and present to judges and mentors across multiple rounds.

02

Flight Schedule

  • 15 - 16 March: Registrations Open
  • 17 March: Problem Statement Submission
  • 26 March: Prototype Development Deadline
  • Event Day: Multiple Pitching Rounds (9:30 AM to 7:00 PM)
03

Evaluation Rounds

  • Round 1 (9:30 AM): Prototype Evaluation (Innovation, Feasibility, Quality)
  • Round 2 (1:00 PM): Risk & Multiplier Pitching Round
  • Finals (5:00 PM): Final Investment Pitch & Winner Selection
