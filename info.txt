Proposal for Conducting The Runway – Innovation & Startup Pitch Event
________________________________________
1. Event Overview
The Runway is a one-day innovation and entrepreneurship event designed to encourage students to develop creative solutions, build prototypes, and present their ideas in a startup-style pitching environment.
Participants will first submit problem statements, from which selected teams will be shortlisted by the CSM department faculty. The shortlisted teams will develop their prototype and present their ideas before judges and mentors. The event includes multiple pitching rounds, evaluation, and an investment-style judging system to identify the best ideas.
The event aims to improve problem-solving skills, innovation, teamwork, business thinking, and presentation abilities among students.
